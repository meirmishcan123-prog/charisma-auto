# Bruce Lee style — video reference for @charisma.il

A "calm warrior wisdom" style: Bruce Lee's philosophy translated into short,
cinematic Hebrew reels. Learned from brucelee.com, his 1971 "Lost Interview",
and analyses of his charisma. Use this as the source of truth when writing
Bruce Lee-style scripts.

## Core themes (each video = ONE theme)

1. **Be water (היה כמו מים)** — adaptability. "Empty your mind. Be formless,
   shapeless, like water. Water can flow, or it can crash."
   Hebrew anchor: "רוקן את הראש. היה כמו מים — מים זורמים, ומים מרסקים."

2. **One kick 10,000 times (משמעת של בעיטה אחת)** — focused practice beats
   variety. "I fear not the man who has practiced 10,000 kicks once, but I
   fear the man who has practiced one kick 10,000 times."
   Hebrew anchor: "אני לא מפחד ממי שתרגל 10,000 בעיטות פעם אחת. אני מפחד ממי שתרגל בעיטה אחת 10,000 פעמים."

3. **Daily decrease (לחתוך את המיותר)** — simplicity. "It's not the daily
   increase but daily decrease. Hack away at the unessential."
   Hebrew anchor: "גדולה היא לא להוסיף כל יום. גדולה היא להוריד כל יום את מה שמיותר."

4. **Knowing is not enough (לדעת זה לא מספיק)** — action. "Knowing is not
   enough, we must apply. Willing is not enough, we must do."
   Hebrew anchor: "לדעת זה לא מספיק, צריך ליישם. לרצות זה לא מספיק, צריך לעשות."

5. **Walk On (תמשיך ללכת)** — resilience. His mantra during his back injury,
   written on a card on his desk: "Walk On!"
   Hebrew anchor: "כשהגב שלו נשבר, הוא כתב על פתק שתי מילים: תמשיך ללכת."

6. **No limits (אין גבולות)** — "If you always put limits on everything you
   do... it will spread into your work and into your life. There are no limits.
   There are only plateaus, and you must not stay there."
   Hebrew anchor: "אם אתה שם גבול על משהו אחד, הוא מתפשט לכל החיים שלך."

7. **Be yourself (תהיה אתה)** — authenticity as the root of charisma. "Always
   be yourself, express yourself, have faith in yourself. Do not go out and
   look for a successful personality and duplicate it."
   Hebrew anchor: "אל תחפש אישיות מצליחה כדי להעתיק אותה. תשאל: איך אני יכול להיות אני?"

8. **Low aim is the crime (הפשע הוא לכוון נמוך)** — "Not failure, but low aim,
   is the crime. In great attempts it is glorious even to fail."
   Hebrew anchor: "הכישלון הוא לא הפשע. לכוון נמוך — זה הפשע."

9. **Self-knowledge (לדעת את עצמך)** — "All knowledge simply means
   self-knowledge." Mistakes are forgivable if one has the courage to admit them.

10. **Master your attitude (אתה שולט בגישה)** — "You have choice. You are
    master of your attitude. Choose the positive, the constructive."

## What made HIM charismatic (use as content ideas too)

- Measured, mid-tempo speech with deliberate PAUSES that build anticipation
- Total conviction — spoke only about what he deeply knew (expertise)
- Authenticity over imitation — never copied anyone's style
- Intensity switches: calm smile → dead-serious focus in a heartbeat
- Passion you could feel — he meant every word

## Script structure for a Bruce Lee-style video (12-13 segments, ~55s)

1. HOOK (2 segments): open with the man, not the lesson. A moment, a paradox
   or a scene: "האיש הכי מהיר בעולם פחד מדבר אחד." / "בשנת 1970 ברוס לי שבר את הגב. הרופאים אמרו: תשכח מקרטה."
2. THE STORY BEAT (2-3 segments): one true, concrete detail from his life
   (the injury + "Walk On" card, the one-kick principle, turning down roles
   that stereotyped him, writing his philosophy notebooks).
3. THE PRINCIPLE (3-4 segments): the ONE theme, translated to simple Hebrew,
   with the anchor quote as the centerpiece segment.
4. THE APPLICATION (2-3 segments): what the viewer does TODAY with it —
   one concrete action, Bruce-style (cut one unessential thing; practice one
   skill 20 minutes; write "תמשיך ללכת" on a note).
5. FEAR + JEALOUSY beats (2 segments) — as always.
6. CTA — unchanged per platform (IG: comment "אני"; TikTok: link in bio).

## Rules

- Quote lines are marked as the emotional peak — keep them SHORT and exact.
- Never invent fake Bruce Lee quotes; use only themes/quotes from this file.
- Tone: calm, quiet power. No hype words. Let the pauses do the work.
- Visuals stay the brand's cinematic nature style (water imagery is a natural
  fit: rivers, ocean, waterfalls for "be water"; mountains for "no limits";
  a single path for "walk on").
- Attribute wisdom to Bruce Lee by name — the authority is part of the value.
